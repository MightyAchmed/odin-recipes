Bratwurst Sausage

This is even moreso just that other sausage.

This breakfast sausage is made with a few pounds of ground pork and a whole mess of seasonings. Throw in some maple syrup for an extra sweet kick!

  • 2 Tbsp. brown sugar, packed
  • 5 tsp. dried sage
  • 5 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/8 tsp. ground cloves
  1. Get some pork, grind it all up real good
  2. Get all of your seasonings together, and some water, mix that with the pork real good
  3. Get some casings and stuff the meat in
  4. Cook and eat
